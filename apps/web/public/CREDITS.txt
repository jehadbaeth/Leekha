Third-party assets bundled with this app
=========================================

Sounds (public/sounds/)
------------------------
Source: Kenney (www.kenney.nl) — "Casino Audio", "Interface Sounds",
"Music Jingles", and "Sci-Fi Sounds" (emote-wow.ogg, an explosion, for the
exploding-head emote) packs.
License: Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal (public domain dedication).
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/
Attribution is not required, but credit to Kenney is appreciated.

Note: emote-nice.ogg (clapping-hands emote) and emote-haha.ogg (laughing
emote) are still generic UI blips, not real applause/laughter recordings —
none of Kenney's CC0 packs include human vocal or crowd sounds. Swapping
these two for the real thing needs an actual licensed clap/laugh sample
(e.g. a specific CC0-tagged freesound.org clip, chosen and licensing-checked
by a person) rather than this app's stock CC0 asset pipeline.

Animated emotes (public/emotes/)
---------------------------------
Source: Google's Noto Emoji Animation set, https://googlefonts.github.io/noto-emoji-animation/
(https://github.com/googlefonts/noto-emoji)
License: Apache License, Version 2.0 for the animated artwork.
https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0
Files used, by Unicode codepoint: 1F44F, 1F602, 1F92F, 1F648, 1F525, 1F64F,
1F629, 1F91D, 1F921, 1F37F.

These are generic, openly-licensed emoji/SFX packs, not another app's
proprietary artwork or sound effects.
